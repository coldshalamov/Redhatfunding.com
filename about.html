Our mission

Empower small businesses with transparent capital so they can hire, restock, and grow on their terms.

From construction crews to ecommerce brands, our team puts reliable working capital within reach. We pair data-driven underwriting with empathetic conversations that respect your time and goals.

Our vision

A world where Main Street founders access capital with confidence—no jargon, no hidden catches.

We are building long-term relationships. When you win, we win. That’s why we focus on education, long-range planning, and staying accessible long after your first funding round.

Our story

RedHat Funding began in 2015 when our founder saw hardworking business owners burned by predatory contracts. We set out to change that narrative with radical transparency, proactive communication, and lenders that compete for your business.

Today we’ve grown into a nationwide brokerage with bilingual funding managers, smart automation, and a network of trusted MCA providers. Yet our promise stays the same: we only recommend offers we’d sign ourselves.
