An MCA provides upfront capital in exchange for a share of your future sales. Instead of a fixed monthly payment, a small percentage of your daily or weekly revenue is remitted until the agreed-upon amount is satisfied.

We regularly see approvals within 24 business hours and funding within 72 hours once the offer is accepted. Same-day options are available when bank data can be verified instantly.

No hard credit pull is required to pre-qualify. Some funding partners may run a soft inquiry before funding, which does not impact your credit score.

Typically we request the most recent three months of business bank statements, a voided business check, and a copy of a valid government-issued ID. Additional documents may be requested depending on the offer you select.

Yes. We work with MCA providers that consider businesses with tax or banking liens and owners with credit scores starting around 500, provided cash flow is healthy.

Repayment is typically a fixed percentage of your daily or weekly card sales or ACH deposits. When business slows, your remittance decreases accordingly. We model holdback percentages so you understand the cash flow impact before you sign.

Yes. Business owners commonly invest in inventory, payroll, marketing, equipment, or new locations. We’ll help you budget so the advance works for today and tomorrow.

We keep in touch with every client. Once you have repaid a portion of your balance, we can review renewal options or additional programs to keep your business moving forward.
